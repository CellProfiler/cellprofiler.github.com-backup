Installation Instructions

On Windows: Download and execute the CellProfilerSetup executable, and follow the prompts. The installer will add CellProfiler to your Programs menu, and a desktop shortcut, if you wish.

On Max OSX: Download and open the CellProfiler PKG, and follow the prompts. The installer will add CellProfiler to your /Applications folder.

On Linux: See this page for instructions.
